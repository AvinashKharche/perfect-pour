## 2. Graphics Assets Guide

You cannot generate these directly in code, but here is exactly what you need to capture/create:

### **App Icon (512x512 px)**
*   **Source:** Use `assets/images/marketing/app_icon_v2.svg`
*   **Description:** A stylized, gradient water drop (Cyan to Purple) on a dark midnight blue background.
*   **Action:** Open the SVG in a browser or editor (like Figma/Inkscape) and export as PNG.

### **Feature Graphic (1024x500 px)**
*   **Description:** A wide banner showing the "Perfect Pour" logo text in the center, with liquid waves at the bottom and floating glass containers on the sides.
*   **Background:** Use the `bgDarkest` color (#0F0E17).

### **Screenshots (Phone & Tablet)**
Take screenshots of the following moments in the game:

1.  **"Precision Gameplay"**: A screenshot of the Game Screen while pouring, showing the liquid level *almost* hitting the target line.
2.  **"Satisfying Results"**: The Result Overlay showing "PERFECT!" with 3 stars and the "New Record" streak flame.
3.  **"100+ Levels"**: The Level Select screen showing the grid of beautiful glass icons.
4.  **"Boss Challenges"**: A screenshot of a Boss Level (Level 10, 20, etc.) showing the purple "BOSS LEVEL" badge.
5.  **"Achievements"**: The Achievement Popup appearing at the top of the screen.

### **Video Preview (Optional)**
*   Record a 30-second clip showing 3 perfect pours in a row to demonstrate the "flow" and ASMR sound effects.
